# Chapter 5 — Election day

**Objective:** detailed reconstruction of polling day. Typically the chapter with the highest density of findings.

**Expected structure (## subsections):**

## 5.1 Day's chronology

- Scheduled and effective polling-station opening
- Incidents during regular hours (08:00-17:00 for PER)
- Exceptional extensions of voting hours and authority ordering them
- Effective closing and transition to tally

## 5.2 ONPE operations and logistical compliance

- Distribution of electoral material: document successes and failures
- Polling-station installation: quorum reached vs. non-installation
- Role of ONPE personnel and party representatives
- STAE technology system in operation (successes and failures by region)

## 5.3 Documented suffrage exclusion

- Specific figure of citizens who could not vote (with citation to primary source)
- Geographic distribution (map visualised separately)
- Attributable cause (ONPE logistics, non-installation, technological failures)
- Specific breach: ICCPR Art. 25(b), ACHR Art. 23(1)(b), Constitution Art. 31

## 5.4 Observation and transparency

- Presence of international observers (for PER: Transparencia, OAS/DECO if mandated, EU EOM if accredited, Carter Center)
- Suspension of Transparencia's quick count if applicable
- **International media coverage**: if there are NO specific Hunter findings on international coverage, do NOT write "was not systematically documented". Instead, document SUMMARILY which international outlets typically cover Peruvian elections (BBC Mundo, Reuters Latin America, AFP in Spanish, AP, EFE, El País International, Le Monde Americas section, NYT) and characterise qualitatively what is expected given the process context. Clarify in parentheses that day-specific coverage will be incorporated in the final report once the Hunter integrates international sources (Sprint Hunter-International, planned).

## 5.5 Polling-station integrity incidents

- Specific manipulation cases (e.g., JNE polling officer in Trujillo destroys ballots)
- In-flagrante arrests
- Clear distinction between operational failures vs. intentional fraud

- Length: **800-1000 words**.
