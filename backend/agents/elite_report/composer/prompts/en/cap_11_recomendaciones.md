# Chapter 11 — Recommendations

**Objective:** actionable matrix of recommendations across 3 time horizons (short/medium/long term) with a specific responsible body.

**Expected structure (## subsections):**

## 11.1 Short term (48 hours – 30 days)

For the immediate post-report period. Each recommendation with this format:

> **R1.1** — {Specific action}. Responsible: {Body}. Timeframe: {days}. Evidence basis: {Ch. N, specific finding}.

Typical short-term recommendation examples:

- Publication of the tally-backlog schedule by ONPE
- Audited public registry of citizens affected by non-installation of polling stations
- Disaggregated technical report on the electoral technology system (STAE)
- Precautionary suspension of officials with serious criminal complaints
- Official statement by international observers

## 11.2 Medium term (1–6 months)

For the post-process electoral cycle. Examples:

- Comprehensive independent audit of technology systems (STAE/SCE/SPR)
- Review of the appointment process for the electoral body head
- Conclusion of specific criminal investigations
- Publication of the source code for the AI component
- Reform of the logistical protocol for distributing electoral material

## 11.3 Long term (6 months – pre-next elections)

For the legislative/structural cycle. Examples:

- **AI in Electoral Processes Law** with standards on audit, explainability, right of appeal over automated decisions, liability regime
- Comprehensive reform of the electoral body (autonomy, resources, governance)
- Strengthening of the campaign finance regime
- Integration with emerging international standards (Council of Europe AI Framework Convention 2024, UNESCO Recommendation on AI Ethics 2021)

## 11.4 Recommendations to the international system

- To the UN HR Committee: include the findings in the next periodic report
- To the IACHR: consider the information in regional monitoring
- To the OAS: evaluate the applicability of the IADC mechanisms
- To cooperation bodies (IDEA, NDI): offer specific technical assistance

**Requirements:**

- Length: **700-900 words**.
- **Every recommendation must be anchored in a specific finding** of the report (explicitly mentioned).
- **Every recommendation must have a named responsible body** (not "the State" but "Congress" / "JNE" / "JNJ" / "Prosecutor's Office" / etc.).
- **Every recommendation must have an estimated timeframe**.
- Matrix format — use lists with consistent structure.
- No generic recommendations of the type "strengthen democracy".

**Tone:** prescriptive but grounded. Official mission recommendation language.
